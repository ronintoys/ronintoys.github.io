About Ruby

The human behind the chaos

The Story So Far

It started with three cats who had very specific opinions about their entertainment. Gwilo wanted something cooperative. Zelda demanded something worthy of her royal status. Crawrdd needed chaos that matched his sound-activated energy.

Traditional toys weren't cutting it. Store-bought options either bored them instantly or lasted about as long as my attention span during a conference call.

So I started making things. Not because I had some grand vision of disrupting the pet industry, but because three very persuasive cats made it clear that their current entertainment options were inadequate.

The Philosophy

"Play is essential and effortless" isn't just a tagline—it's what I learned watching my cats. They don't need instructions or learning objectives. They need things that respond to their natural instincts and then get out of the way.

👤

Ruby

aka @ruphilka

Professional cat servant, occasional human

The Mission

Create toys that break beautifully, engage naturally, and respect the intelligence of both animals and their humans. No flashing lights, no forced "learning outcomes"—just thoughtful design that lets play be play.

Field Notes from the Research Department

Our design consultants operate under the assumption that humans are reasonably intelligent but need constant supervision

🐱
Cooperative Hunter

Subject: Gwilo

Age: ~8 Earth Years • Status: Senior Research Fellow in Applied Mischief

Primary Research Focus: Gwilo operates on the revolutionary principle that cooperation between species is not only possible but essential for maximum chaos achievement. Currently investigating the correlation between teamwork and fun.

Notable Discovery: Cute aggression as a valid expression of overwhelming affection. Sometimes subjects become so overwhelmed by love that they must attack the object of their affection "just a little bit." Endearing and mildly terrifying.

Research Philosophy: "If it doesn't invite participation, what's the point?"

Current Project: Reelin

An organic fishing apparatus developed in collaboration with Gwilo. Made from bamboo that grew up wanting to be a fishing rod, paired with cotton string that has commitment issues.

Technical Specifications: Designed to achieve Beautiful Destruction after approximately 50 sessions of quality chaos. Comes with a 3-month warranty because we believe in second chances (and Gwilo believes in third and fourth chances).

"Finally, a human who understands that playing together is the point." —Gwilo

Current Project: Rover

A modified micro mouse engineered to Zelda's exacting specifications for live prey simulation. Her research focuses on the precise mathematics of pursuit—the exact angle of approach, the optimal unpredictability ratio, the perfect moment of engagement.

Research Methodology: Zelda approaches hunting as high art, demanding nothing less than perfection in every movement pattern. She evaluates each autonomous sequence with the cool detachment of a master critic, dismissing inferior entertainment with elegant disdain.

"It will suffice. For now." —Zelda (The highest possible endorsement)

👑
Chief Perfectionist

Subject: Zelda

Age: 7.5 Earth Years • Status: Senior Research Fellow in Applied Precision

Primary Research Focus: The mathematical perfection of live prey simulation. Zelda studies optimal hunting sequences with the detached precision of a master theorist, demanding nothing less than flawless execution in every movement pattern.

Research Methodology: Approaches each hunting scenario as high art, evaluating autonomous prey behavior with cool, professional distance. Dismisses substandard entertainment with elegant indifference, reserving approval only for work that meets her exacting standards.

Notable Contribution: Established the principle that nonchalance and perfection are not mutually exclusive—true mastery appears effortless precisely because it is so refined.

Research Philosophy: "If it cannot achieve perfection, it has no business existing in my vicinity."

👁️
Chaos Specialist

Subject: Crawrdd

Age: 1.9 Earth Years • Status: Mr. Cloud, Velocity Research Fellow

Primary Research Focus: Sound-activated velocity studies. Demonstrates the fascinating phenomenon where contemplative zen-master energy instantly transforms into pure kinetic chaos upon auditory stimulation.

Current Hypothesis: The universe contains exactly two states—"dreamy contemplation" and "EVERYTHING IS HAPPENING RIGHT NOW." No middle ground has been observed.

Notable Contribution to Science: Proved that puzzle toys are actually meditation devices in disguise. Round eyes that seem to take in everything at once support this theory.

Research Philosophy: "Everything is interesting! Wait, what was I doing? Oh right, that thing is MOVING!"

Current Project: Rollin' Ronin

A weighted apparatus that creates unpredictable movement patterns based on Crawrdd's research into chaos-focus correlation. Some minds need randomness to engage rather than being overwhelmed.

Technical Innovation: No batteries, no complicated mechanisms. Just physics having philosophical disagreements with itself, creating sound-triggered movement that activates zoom mode while engaging puzzle-solving cognitive processes.

"Wait, did it just—OH MY GOD IT MOVED!" —Crawrdd, every single time
